## Summary

This project studies high-dimensional observations generated from low-dimensional latent factors through a nonlinear map, informally written as `X = g(f) + u`. The goal is to understand when a linear projection can preserve enough information about the latent factors.

## Methods

Manifold assumptions, nonlinear factor models, PCA, secant geometry, autoencoder-style reconstruction objectives.

## Status

Research notes and theoretical development.
