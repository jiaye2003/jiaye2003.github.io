## Summary

This project develops and evaluates CTA-style futures momentum strategies. The strategy combines time-series momentum and cross-sectional momentum signals, applies volatility scaling, and incorporates transaction cost assumptions.

## Methods

Time-series momentum, cross-sectional momentum, volatility targeting, long-short portfolio construction, backtesting.

## Status

Quantitative research project.
