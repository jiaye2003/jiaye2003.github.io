## Summary

This project studies LATE estimation under high-dimensional covariates. The focus is on nuisance-function estimation, identification assumptions, simulation design, and asymptotic analysis.

## Methods

Instrumental variables, LATE, nuisance estimation, high-dimensional statistics, neural-network-based estimation.
